About Diseases In This App

Heart Failure

Heart failure can occur if the heart cannot pump (systolic) or fill (diastolic) adequately.
Symptoms : shortness of breath, fatigue, swollen legs and rapid heartbeat.
Treatments : eating less salt, limiting fluid intake and taking prescription medication.

Malaria

A disease caused by a Plasmodium parasite, transmitted by the bite of infected Female Anopheles mosquitoes.
Symptoms : chills, fever and sweating, usually occurring a few weeks after being bitten.
Treatments : Treatment aims to eliminate the Plasmodium parasite from the bloodstream.

Tuberculosis

A potentially serious infectious bacterial disease that mainly affects the lungs.
Symptoms : usually include a cough, weight loss, night sweats and fever.
Treatments : Patients with active symptoms will require a long course of treatment involving multiple antibiotics.
